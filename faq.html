Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our parts, ordering, shipping, and warranty.

Ordering & Parts
How do I search for a part?
Use our search tool on the homepage or any parts page. Select your vehicle's year, make, model, and the part you need. We'll search our network of 2,000+ yards and get back to you within 24 hours with available options and pricing.
Are the parts OEM or aftermarket?
All parts sourced through AUAPW are genuine OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts that have been removed from salvage vehicles. We do not sell aftermarket parts - only quality used OEM parts.
How do I know if a part will fit my vehicle?
Our team verifies compatibility before providing a quote. We check VIN compatibility, interchange numbers, and manufacturer specifications to ensure the part will fit your specific vehicle.
Can I request parts for older or rare vehicles?
Yes! Our network includes specialty yards that focus on classic, vintage, and rare vehicles. Submit a quote request and our team will search our extended network for hard-to-find parts.
Shipping & Delivery
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location and the part's origin. Expedited shipping options are available for an additional fee. Large items like engines and transmissions may take 5-10 business days.
Do you ship to all 50 states?
Yes, we ship to all 50 states. Parts ship directly from our partner yards to your door. Shipping costs vary based on part size, weight, and destination.
Can I track my order?
Yes, once your order ships you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your shipment directly through the carrier's website.
Warranty & Returns
What warranty do parts come with?
All parts come with a standard 6-month warranty covering defects and functionality issues. Some high-value parts like engines and transmissions may have extended warranty options available.
What if the part doesn't work?
If a part is defective or doesn't function properly, contact us within 6 months for a replacement or refund. We'll arrange return shipping and send a replacement part at no additional cost.
Can I return a part if it doesn't fit?
If a part doesn't fit despite our compatibility verification, contact us within 30 days. We'll work with you to find the correct part or issue a refund minus return shipping costs.
Payment & Pricing
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for large orders. Payment is processed securely through our encrypted checkout system.
Are your prices negotiable?
Our prices are already competitive since we connect you directly with yards. However, for bulk orders or repeat customers, we may be able to offer additional discounts. Contact us to discuss.
Is there a core charge?
Some parts like alternators, starters, and certain engine components may have a core charge that's refunded when you return your old part. Core charges are clearly stated in the quote.
